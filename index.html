About the Conference

The CEPR-JIE Conference on Applied Industrial Organization is an annual conference organized in collaboration with the Centre for Economic Policy Research (CEPR) and the Journal of Industrial Economics. The 26th edition is hosted by LUISS University in Rome and funded by the European Research Council (ERC).
